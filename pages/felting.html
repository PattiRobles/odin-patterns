Felting

What is felting?

Felting is a textile art that involves compacting and matting fibrous materials together to create a dense, warm, and flat fabric. There are multiple methods for felting, including needle felting and wet felting, the later being the technique we are interested in when it comes to knitting.

Wet felting

This method uses hot water and sometimes soap to agitate and compress layers of wool, causing the fibers to hook together. Wet felting is good for making clothing, bags, and other decorative items

Steps for wet felting

  • select feltable yarn to complete project
  • complete knitting project
  • hot water wash cycle in washing machine, at least 40 degrees
  • check and repeat if the stitches can still be seen or the item is stretchy
  • rinse in a cold cycle and spin at 800rpm max
  • shape item and fully dry
